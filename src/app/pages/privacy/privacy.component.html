Summary

This inventory management application prioritises simplicity and ease of use while incorporating fundamental security measures through Angular's built-in protections and TypeScript's type safety. For a production deployment, additional security layers including authentication, server-side validation, encrypted storage, and comprehensive audit logging would be necessary to fully protect sensitive inventory and business data.

Regular security audits, dependency updates, and adherence to the OWASP Top 10 guidelines are recommended practices for maintaining a secure application over time.
